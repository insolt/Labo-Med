Welcome on LABO-MED's website

Our company is one of the biggest medical analysis laboratory in Wielkopolska voivodshaft. We make thousands of different kinds of analytical and microbiological tests every day. All of them you will find on our website. If you want to check any parameter of your blood or urine, it should visit us. Before caming try to find some information about how to prepare yourself for tests.

Analytical Laboratory LABO-MED® is a automated and modern facility, performing many different types of tests in the field of hematology, analytics, bacteriology, allergy, immunology, biochemistry, virology, and many others. We also perform tests to detect various types of psychoactive substances such as alcohol or drugs.

Our main goal is continuously to improve service's quality using the latest technology and continuously train our staff.
